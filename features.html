What Makes AriZona great?

Info

Arizona drinks offer a wide variety of bold and refreshing flavors, some popular options like Green Tea with Ginseng and Honey, Mucho Mango, and Fruit Punch. Each drink comes in a large 28 ML can, They are bright, colourful designs that make them stand out on the shelf. The brand is especially known for its low price, often just 1.75$ - 2$, which has become a signature part of its identity. Many of the drinks are made with real sugar and natural ingredients, giving them a more authentic taste compared to other sugary drinks. Some Arizona drinks are also caffeine-free or come in low-calorie versions, making them a flexible for different lifestyles. With their great taste, and unique packaging, Arizona drinks remain a favorite among everyone.

List of ingredients

  • filtered water
  • tea extracts
  • black, green, or decaf
  • sweeteners like sugar or high-fructose corn syrup
  • citric or ascorbic acid
